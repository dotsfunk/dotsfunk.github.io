Welcome to the DFO Website!

We are DFO and we are here to tell you that we funk for money! Dot’s Funk Odyssey (DFO to our friends) are a 14 piece funk band based in Oxford, UK. We love to perform, and we’re ideal for getting audiences dancing, wiggling and having a good time!

We play at many types of event, including balls, charity gigs, festivals and private functions. Playing everything from Stevie Wonder to Cee-Lo Green and from Aretha Franklin to Chaka Khan and Mark Ronson, our constantly updated set lists are guaranteed to be packed with songs that audiences just love to dance to.
